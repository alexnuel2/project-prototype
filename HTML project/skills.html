Cyber Security

What is Cyber Security?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting normal business processes.

How to Learn Cyber Security

Learning cybersecurity involves a combination of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience. Below are steps to get started:

  • Understand the fundamentals of IT and networking.
  • Learn about different types of threats like malware, phishing, and ransomware.
  • Familiarize yourself with tools like firewalls, antivirus software, and encryption methods.
  • Take online courses and certifications like CompTIA Security+, CEH, or CISSP.
  • Join cybersecurity communities and forums for networking and knowledge-sharing.

Types & Categories of Cyber Security

  • Network Security: Protects the integrity of a network and its data from intrusions and threats.
  • Information Security: Protects sensitive data from unauthorized access and modification.
  • Application Security: Secures software applications from threats during development and deployment.
  • Cloud Security: Focuses on protecting data stored and processed in the cloud.
  • Endpoint Security: Protects individual devices like laptops, smartphones, and tablets from cyberattacks.
